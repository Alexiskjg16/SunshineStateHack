Welcome to Hack the Crisis!

June 18-21, 2020

What is it?

An online event where you meet with other creatives, developers, and health care workers who all have a common goal of using technology to solve the problems we face related to the current pandemic. Problem sets are not limited to the health care field. You are encouraged to think broadly when deciding on a challenge your team would like to solve. Solutions can address global or local issues. Some ideas to get you started, but in no way are limited to health, vulnerable populations, small local business, community, education, entertainment, beaches, and parks.
